This is a glance at the forecast, not a flight plan. Where a TAF exists it's used and shown in full, but it knows nothing about NOTAMs, airspace, PPR or runway state, and small strips have no TAF at all, so those fall back to a general weather model. Read the raw TAF, check NOTAMs, and re-check on the day before you commit to anything.
